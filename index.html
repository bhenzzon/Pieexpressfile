PIE EXPRESS

MAKING A HEALTHIER PHILIPPINES, ONE KUCHAY PIE AT A TIME! WELCOME TO OUR HEALTH REVOLUTION!

PIE EXPRESS is in the business of selling Kuchay Pies which uses tofu and vegetables as a meat substitute and base ingredients that are considered as Vegan or Plant based product.

PIE EXPRESS started operating in April 2020 via an online store with just the owners themselves, they serve thousands of orders to various markets.

Now, PIE EXPRESS expands its variety of products and now offers a variety of dimsums, meat pies, and fruitchiayakultea that are still healthy and delicious.

With the positive reviews of the products, PIE EXPRESS now has physical outlets in the metro and soon with other neighboring cities.
